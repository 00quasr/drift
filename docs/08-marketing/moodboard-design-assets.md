## **Design Elements & Assets**

### **1. Geometric Patterns**
- **Angular Corner Brackets**: L-shaped borders for framing
- **Grid Overlays**: 20px x 20px cyan grid patterns
- **Dot Matrix**: Interactive particle systems
- **Scan Lines**: Horizontal CRT-style lines

### **2. Visual Effects**
- **Glassmorphism**: backdrop-blur-3xl with transparency
- **Neon Glow**: text-shadow and box-shadow effects
- **Grain Texture**: SVG noise filters at 3% opacity
- **Motion Blur**: Glitch and pulse animations

### **3. Interface Components**
- **Cards**: Black backgrounds with white/20% borders
- **Buttons**: Angular, uppercase, tracking-wide
- **Tags**: Minimal borders with neon accents
- **Search**: Real-time with categorized results

## **Brand Guidelines for Social Media**

### **Do's**
✅ Use pure black backgrounds consistently  
✅ Maintain uppercase typography for headers  
✅ Apply subtle neon accents sparingly  
✅ Include angular geometric elements  
✅ Preserve white space for minimalism  
✅ Use Inter font family exclusively  

### **Don'ts**
❌ Overuse neon colors (max 2 per asset)  
❌ Use rounded corners (maintain angular aesthetic)  
❌ Include complex gradients  
❌ Compromise text readability  
❌ Deviate from established color palette  
❌ Use lowercase in primary headings  

---

## **Motion & Animation Guidelines**

### **Micro-Animations**
- **Duration**: 0.3-0.8 seconds
- **Easing**: ease-out, ease-in-out
- **Effects**: Fade, slide, scale (1.02x max)
- **Hover States**: Subtle glow increase

### **Loading States**
- **Pulse**: 2-3 second cycles
- **Spin**: For search/loading indicators
- **Fade**: Smooth content transitions
- **Scale**: Breathing effects at 1-1.02 scale

### **Social Media Video**
- **Length**: 15-30 seconds maximum
- **Framerate**: 30fps
- **Resolution**: Platform-specific optimized
- **Audio**: Optional ambient electronic music
