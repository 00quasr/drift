## ğŸµ Electronic Music Aesthetic Guidelines

### Visual Elements

#### Waveforms and Audio Visualizations
- **Usage**: Background elements, headers, dividers
- **Style**: Clean, minimalist waveforms
- **Colors**: Neon accents on dark backgrounds
- **Animation**: Subtle movement for video content

#### Neon Accent Integration
- **Primary Neon**: Electric green (#00ff88)
- **Secondary Neon**: Hot pink (#ff0066)
- **Tertiary Neon**: Cyan blue (#00ccff)
- **Usage**: Highlights, CTAs, important information
- **Balance**: 10-15% of total color usage

#### Dark Background Strategies
- **Pure Black**: #000000 for maximum contrast
- **Rich Black**: #1a1a1a for softer appearance
- **Gradient Blacks**: Subtle transitions for depth
- **Texture Overlays**: Subtle noise or patterns

#### Geometric Shape Integration
- **Style**: Clean, angular shapes
- **Usage**: Frames, borders, background elements
- **Alignment**: Grid-based, precise positioning
- **Purpose**: Visual hierarchy and organization

#### Typography Specifications
**Headers:**
- Font: Bold sans-serif (Montserrat Bold, Roboto Bold)
- Weight: 700-900
- Spacing: Tight letter spacing (-0.02em)
- Transform: Uppercase for impact

**Body Text:**
- Font: Clean sans-serif (Open Sans, Roboto Regular)
- Weight: 400-500
- Spacing: Normal letter spacing
- Line Height: 1.5-1.6 for readability

**Tech Elements:**
- Font: Monospace (Courier, Monaco, Roboto Mono)
- Usage: Code snippets, data, timestamps
- Weight: 400
- Purpose: Technical authenticity

### Photography Style Guidelines

#### Club/Venue Photography
- **Lighting**: Dramatic, atmospheric lighting
- **Composition**: Wide shots showing space and energy
- **Colors**: Saturated neon colors, deep shadows
- **Focus**: Environmental storytelling
- **Mood**: Electric, energetic, immersive

#### Artist Portraits
- **Style**: Professional but authentic
- **Lighting**: Clean, even lighting with dramatic accents
- **Background**: Simple, non-distracting backgrounds
- **Composition**: Rule of thirds, strong eye contact
- **Processing**: High contrast, selective color grading

#### Equipment and Technical Shots
- **Focus**: DJ setups, sound systems, technical details
- **Lighting**: Clean, technical lighting
- **Composition**: Detail shots and wide context shots
- **Style**: Professional, gear-focused
- **Purpose**: Technical credibility and expertise

#### Crowd and Community Photography
- **Energy**: Movement, dancing, engagement
- **Composition**: Wide shots and intimate moments
- **Lighting**: Available light, authentic atmosphere
- **Privacy**: Respect for individual privacy
- **Purpose**: Community and connection emphasis
